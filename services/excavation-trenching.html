Safe Excavation & Trenching in Simpsonville, SC

Excavation and trenching in Simpsonville entail considerable safety hazards, such as potential damage to utility lines or the risk of cave-ins. At Dream Green Land Clearing and Grading, we prioritize safety by employing a professional methodology that conforms to OSHA standards. Our process begins with thorough pre-digging assessments, which include contacting 811 to mark underground utilities, thereby ensuring a secure excavation experience.

Equipped with state-of-the-art machinery, we are capable of operating efficiently and safely across various conditions. Proudly serving the Simpsonville area, including Greenville, we specialize in utility trenching for water, sewer, gas, or electric lines, as well as foundation and footing excavation, providing you with accurate and dependable results.

Our quick quotes and ability to commence work within 2–4 weeks keep your project on schedule. With our satisfaction guarantee, you can have confidence in our ability to fulfill your excavation requirements.
