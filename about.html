Who We Are

About Dream Green

17+ years of professional land clearing, grading, and site preparation in Simpsonville, SC.

Our Story

Build on a Foundation of Excellence

Dream Green Land Clearing and Grading was founded with a single purpose: to help Simpsonville-area property owners unlock the full potential of their land. Over the past 17+ years, we have built a reputation for quality, reliability, and integrity that our community depends on.

Whether you are a homeowner preparing a lot for a new home, a developer clearing acreage for a subdivision, or a contractor needing precise grading and excavation, we bring the expertise and equipment to get it done right — on time and on budget.

We are fully licensed and insured, committed to safety, and deeply knowledgeable about the local terrain, soil types, and regulations that affect your project.

Our Core Services

🌿

Land Clearing

Transforming overgrown or wooded areas into usable, clean spaces ready for development.

Grading & Leveling
