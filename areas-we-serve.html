Deep Roots in Upstate South Carolina

Dream Green Land Clearing and Grading is based in Simpsonville and has served the greater upstate South Carolina region for over 17 years. Our team understands the local terrain, soil composition, regulations, and seasonal conditions that affect land clearing and grading projects in this part of the state.

From the rolling hills outside Greenville to the rural properties in Woodruff and Gray Court, we've worked on land of every type and size. Whether you're a homeowner clearing a residential lot or a developer preparing a large commercial site, we bring local expertise and state-of-the-art equipment to every job.
